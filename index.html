About us

Crowd-Time story is a platform to create unique bed-time stories for your kids. You can add many voices to your children's bedtime story choosing from thousands of our users. Parents who are worried about the superficiality, you have nothing to worry! You can be join the creative process of story-telling. By being a character in the story, help your child get an authentic story-telling experience.
