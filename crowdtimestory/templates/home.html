About us

Crowd-Time story is a platform to create unique bed-time stories for your kids. Add multiple voices to your stories and bring life to the characters. Parents can also join the creative process of story-telling by being a character in the story. With our website, you can help your child get a unique and authentic story-telling experience!!

Instructions

Step1: Capture the pictures of all pages of your picture book and name the files in the following format. Please save them to a local directory in your computer.
eg : YOURSTORYNAME_pagenumber

Step2: Upload the image files to our server here.

Step3: Enter a brief desciption of which type of voices you require for your story.For example: male/female, light/heavy etc. /

Step4: Please allow us a day and check back our website for your finished audiobook. We'll create a beautiful story with your audio and present to you in an interactive way.

Finally, listen to the stories with your children and enjoy.
